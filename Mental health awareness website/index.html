Mental Health Matters

Mental health refers to a person's overall psychological and emotional well-being. It encompasses factors such as mood, behavior, and thinking patterns. Good mental health allows individuals to function effectively in their daily lives, manage stress, build positive relationships, and make meaningful contributions to their communities. Mental health can be influenced by a range of factors, including genetics, life experiences, and environmental factors. Mental health disorders can range from mild to severe, and may require professional treatment and support. Raising awareness about mental health is important in reducing the stigma associated with mental illness and promoting access to resources and care.
